So What is This About?

We need to realize that humans strive for greatness. It can be considered a sin, to always want more, never being contempt. But reality is humans want to evolve, in more ways than one. And technology allows us just that, to be healthier, stronger, to travel faster, to see things we can't otherwise. To experience so many things outside out initial existance.

“If it keeps up, man will atrophy all his limbs but the push-button finger.”
Frank Lloyd Wright, Architect

“Once a new technology rolls over you, if you're not part of the steamroller, you're part of the road.”
Stewart Brand, Writer

“Technology is a useful servant but a dangerous master.”

Epilogue

Everything in this world is dual in nature. Every experience, every action, every thought.
Technology brings with it amazing, good things. But also carries with it concequences.
Newton stated that every action has an equal and opposite reaction. I think it is true with "good" and "evil". If most things in your daily life are automated, you are susceptible to become lazy. That's one of many examples. We should thread with care.
